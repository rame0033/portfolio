Closed Focus/ Macro

The goal of the topic is to apply concepts of exposure, composition, focus, depth of field and perspective to a still life image.

A bike minature was used in this assignment. Before the shoot, I decided not to use as much lighting equipment as possible, so I took a shot outside, also used a 50 mm prime lens for this one because it gives much background blur, so the focus will be on the bike itself. It is a fun assignment, and I was able to utilize natural light when taking photos of this subject.
