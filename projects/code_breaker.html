About the project

We are tasked with creating a movie poster for our final project, it could be any genre of choice, but we are required to be in the poster.

Before actual creation, individuals have to think of three words that emphasize the idea of the poster. I thought of the mystery, quiet, and masks. I chose sci-fi as the genre of my movie poster, and think that I could use the emergence of AI as an added concept of my poster.

As shown in the picture below, is the placement of the objects before making certain adjustments to the poster.

In the output, use of Photoshop with the concepts of blur, free transform, and shadow and highlighs adjustment was able to demonstrate. Here's a view of the poster without the graphics so you can clearly see the difference.
