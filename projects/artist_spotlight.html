The Artist Spotlight (2021) aims to promote the artist following their debut in Off The Record, in the other hand, this will also give the audience what to expect next to their favorite artist (e.g. new song release, album release).

Jensen Gomez - Phases Vol. 2

Jensen Gomez - a Filipino songwriter, producer, arranger, and musical scorer for over 14 years. After 8 years of hiatus, he was set to release the second part of the "Phases" trilogy.

The theme of the video is inspired from the album art of the Phases - Vol. 2. The cover has a picture of a console similar to Nintendo 65 with two joysticks - so every track in the EP is collaborative.

As you have observed in the video, every aspect including the transition-in for graphics, sound effects, video overlays are in retro video game vibes.
