To the stars
Cover art

ASTER The Band - a Filipino indie-rock band which consist of Andrew, Calvin, and David will be releasing their first extended play (EP) entitled 'To The Stars.' I was tasked to create a cover art.

The visual has the shades of blue to keep the branding of ASTER in space (literally). The environment is adapted fromthe resources used for their music video "Destiny"- that is originally created by the director, William Capistrano, with the sketch of the band members sitting and watching over the sky.
